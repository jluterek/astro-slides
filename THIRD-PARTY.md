## Notes on non-permissive / attribution licenses

- **`@img/sharp-libvips-*` (LGPL-3.0-or-later)** — prebuilt native `libvips` binaries used
  by `sharp` for build-time image processing (via Astro). Consumed as separate, dynamically
  linked binaries and not redistributed inside the published `@astro-slides/*` tarballs.
- **`@fontsource-variable/inter`, `@fontsource/space-grotesk` (OFL-1.1)** — the Cosmic
  theme's self-hosted typefaces. The SIL Open Font License permits bundling and
  redistribution with attribution; the fonts are not sold on their own.
- **`caniuse-lite` (CC-BY-4.0)** — browser-support data (build-time, via browserslist).
- **`khroma` (license unspecified in its manifest; published as MIT upstream)** — a color
  utility pulled in transitively by Mermaid.
- **`dompurify` (MPL-2.0 OR Apache-2.0)**, **`jszip` (MIT OR GPL-3.0-or-later)**,
  **`pako` (MIT AND Zlib)** — dual/combined licenses; used here under their permissive terms.
